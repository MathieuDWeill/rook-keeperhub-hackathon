# 90-second pitch

Construction payments are broken in both directions. Clients fear paying before the work is complete; artisans lose cash while waiting for validation. Rook turns the quote into a programmable payment mandate.

The client funds once. For each milestone, the agent gathers signed acceptance, invoice and compliance evidence. A deterministic policy engine—not the language model—decides whether release is allowed. KeeperHub then handles the last mile: simulation, reliable execution and an auditable on-chain transaction. A contractual retention can remain locked until final acceptance.

The demo shows both sides: a valid milestone is paid through KeeperHub, while a request with missing acceptance is blocked before any transaction is submitted.

After the hackathon, Rook becomes the secure-payments module of iArtisanat, an operating platform for construction professionals. Revenue comes from a monthly add-on, a fee per protected payment, and a white-label API for marketplaces and financial partners.
